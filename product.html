About the App

LifeGPA is an app that works with you to achieve your goals. As you work toward your goals, you are given regular progress updates that show where you excel and which areas need more attention. Don't worry- we'll help you make the grade.

Focus on what matters. All goals are fully customizable from the goal itself, to the frequency and weight it holds on your overall letter grade. We understand that some goals are more important than others. Whatever your goals may be, find out how we can help you achieve them.
